About Me

Isaac in a Nutshell

I'm an introvert, but that doens't mean I'm a bad communicator, it just means I'm not the life of the party. I learned the value of communication at agile teams for several years.

Okay, so what makes you have long casual conversations then? Talking about history or the tiny little details that connect one culture with another, or one music genre with another. I love observing and noticing interesting facts.

Alright, and what music do you like? Punk Rock, I intrinsecally prefer simple things over complex ones: Punk Rock over Heavy Metal, Cartoons over Realism, minimalist clothing over flashy one, etc. And I naturally bring that into my designs, which at the same time makes them more digestable and easier to understand.

Okay, but what if you can't achieve that so easily, what keeps you pushing through? The idea of making something new, original and innovative, or just graphic work that captures the attention of people. It motivates me! it fuels me!

I got you, and what do you get obsessed with? Clear explanations, I imagine the way people would interpret what I design so I try to do it in the clearest possible way. But now we have UX techniques that help us with that, they're very handy.
